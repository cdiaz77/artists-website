Junior H


Julissa Cisneros and Citlali Diaz

Junior H, who's real name is Antonio Herrera Perez, is a well-known Mexican singer-songwriter. He was born on April 23,2000 which would make him 23 years old as of February 2024. Junior is a native of Cerano, Guanajuato, Mexico. However, at the age of 15, he and his family made the move to the state of Utah. Junior H is best known for his music in the corridos tumbados genre. Currently, his most popular song is Lady Gaga with Peso Pluma and Gabito Ballesteros. He recently celebrated his highest debut on the Billboard's Top Latin Albums at Number 2 with his most recent album, $ad Boyz 4 Life II in 2023.
